Rules and Regulations

Welcome to M.B.S International School. Please adhere to the following rules and regulations:

  • Respect for all staff and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Uniforms must be worn at all times during school hours.
  • No mobile phones or electronic devices are allowed in class.
  • Students must adhere to the school timetable and attend all classes regularly.

  • Follow all safety protocols as instructed by the school administration.
